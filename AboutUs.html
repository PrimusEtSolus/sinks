About our Company

Sink R Us is a leading provider of high-quality kitchen and bathroom sinks. Based in Baguio City,Benguet , we have been serving customers for 69 years. Our commitment to excellence is evident in our wide range of products, including stainless steel, porcelain, and composite sinks. We offer a variety of styles and sizes to suit any taste and budget. Whether you're looking for a modern minimalist design or a classic farmhouse style, Sink R Us has the perfect sink for your home. Our knowledgeable staff is dedicated to helping you find the ideal sink for your needs and providing exceptional customer service.
